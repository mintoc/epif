Stanford Heart Transplantation Programme - 103 patients

Description

The file contains the data on 103 patients, consisting of the patient's age at transplantation, prior open-heart surgery (1 = yes, 0 = no), a censoring indicator (1 = yes, 0 = no), the survival time in days after transplant , a score representing the mismatch between the patient's and the donor's tissue type (values range from 0.00 to 3.05), and an indicator for death by rejection (1 = yes, 0 = no). One zero survival time is recoded to 0.5. There are 41 deaths and 24 censored survivals, with 39 distinct death times.

Usage

stan

Format

A data.frame of 103 obs. of 14 variables:
[,] birth.dt integer,
[,] accept.dt integer,
[,] tx.date integer,
[,] fu.date integer,
[,] fustat integer,
[,] surgery integer, a score representing mismatch of tissue types
[,] age integer, in years
[,] futime integer,
[,] wait.time integer,
[,] transplant Factor w/ 2 levels "TRUE", "FALSE"
[,] mismatch integer, NA NA 2 3 NA NA 4 NA NA 2 ...
[,] hla.a2 integer, NA NA 0 0 NA NA 0 NA NA 0 ...
[,] mscore numeric, NA NA 1.11 1.66 NA NA 1.32 NA NA 0.61 ...
[,] reject integer, NA NA 0 0 NA NA 1 NA NA 1 ...
